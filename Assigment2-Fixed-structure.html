Spring 2026 Favorite Fishing Spots

Spot 1: Guarantee Hole

Guarantee Hole is one of my self-discovered spots. It is only fishable from late fall to early spring, before spring runoff begins and Lookout Point Reservoir fills up. To get there, take highway 58 East from Eugene. After about 35 minutes of driving, you will see a small pull-off area on the right and a Trailhead sign for Eula Ridge. Park here and then proceed down to the little creek. Follow the creek through the tunnel that goes under 58 and pop out right at the hole. Fish a dry dropper and through your first cast close to the bank and you'll be guaranteed a bite.

Spot 2: Secret Run

Secret Run is a productive run that is just downstream of Secret Campground off of Forest Road 197. Laying a few miles above HillsCreek Dam the trout found here are likely Cutthroat Rainbow hybrids. These fish are likely smaller on average than those found at Guarantee Hole but they put up a great fight and are not shy. I first fished here during a march brown hatch and had fish surfacing right and left of me. During hatches I'd fish a high floating dry with either a wet-fly or emerge trailing behind. Without a hatch a nymph rig should be productive. I haven't tried any streamers here but I imagine they might work well.

Spot 3: Big Swamp Spring Creek

Big Swamp Spring Creek is a beautiful remote spot with no fish. I fished here for the better part of three hours and had zero bites, or rises. The water was crystal clear and even at the fishiest spot I couldn't see a single fish. I image that there just isn't enough food to support a fish population here and that better living it further downstream. However, this is a picturesque spot and was well worth the 1.5 hour drive and then 2 mile bushwack to get here. Located about a mile in half east of the eastern most trailhead for the Middle Fork Willamette Trail Big Swamp is stands a grove of old growth trees. With a relatively clear understory within the forest and practically open meadow near the creek this is a spot worth venturing back to.
